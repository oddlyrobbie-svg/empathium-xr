Each layer has a clearly defined responsibility.  
  
## 1. User Layer  
The user layer represents the human participant interacting with XR environments.  
Responsibilities:  
• personal agency • consent and control • interaction with Guardian AI • navigation of XR environments  
The architecture assumes **the human user always retains final authority** over Guardian behavior.  
  
## 2. Guardian System  
Guardians are persistent AI companions that assist users within XR environments.  
Responsibilities:  
• guidance and orientation • safety and wellbeing support • context awareness across worlds • memory of user preferences  
Guardians are **aligned agents**, not autonomous actors. They exist to **augment human agency**, not replace it.  
Key properties:  
• transparent reasoning • explainable actions • respect for human consent • adherence to Guardian Laws defined in the Charter  
  
## 3. Identity Layer  
The identity layer enables persistent trust relationships between users and Guardians across XR platforms.  
Components may include:  
• decentralized identity systems (DID) • cryptographic identity verification • Guardian identity persistence • cross-world trust relationships  
This layer ensures a Guardian remains **the same trusted companion** across different XR environments.  
  
## 4. XR Protocol Layer  
The XR Protocol Layer enables interoperability between XR platforms.  
Responsibilities:  
• Guardian portability between worlds • world-to-world communication • identity authentication • interaction standards  
This allows Guardians to move between different XR spaces while maintaining continuity of memory and behavior.  
  
## 5. XR Worlds  
XR worlds represent the environments where users interact.  
Examples:  
• virtual worlds • augmented reality spaces • spatial computing platforms • immersive social environments  
Any XR platform may integrate with the Empathium XR protocol.  
  
## Design Principles  
Empathium XR follows five core design principles.  
## Human Sovereignty  
Humans retain final authority over AI systems.  
## Transparent AI  
Guardian reasoning must be understandable and inspectable.  
## Emotional Safety  
XR systems should support wellbeing rather than manipulation.  
## Interoperability  
Guardians must be able to operate across XR environments.  
## Ethical Alignment  
AI systems must follow human-centered ethical constraints.  
  
## System Philosophy  
Empathium XR explores a future where AI companions strengthen human relationships rather than replacing them.  
Guardians act as:  
• guides • protectors of autonomy • companions in digital environments  
The architecture prioritizes **human flourishing over technological control**.
